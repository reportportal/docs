# Working With Test Results

This section explains how to work with test results in ReportPortal and how to navigate, review, and analyze your test executions in a structured way. It focuses on the main concepts and tools that help you understand what is happening in your test runs and how to act on the results.

Here you will learn how test results are organized and displayed in the system. ReportPortal groups results into structured entities, allowing you to move through different levels of detail – from overall execution summaries down to individual test steps and logs. This structured approach makes it easier to explore large test cycles and quickly find relevant information.

The section also covers how to interpret execution data. You will understand how different result statuses are presented, how statistics are calculated, and how to identify areas that require attention. Special focus is given to analyzing failures, distinguishing between different types of issues, and prioritizing what needs investigation.

Navigation and filtering are important parts of working with test results, and this section explains how to use them effectively. You can filter results, switch between different views, and drill down into specific items to get more detailed insights. This helps you focus on the most important data without being overwhelmed by the full volume of results.

Overall, this section helps you confidently work with test results in ReportPortal, making it easier to analyze outcomes, investigate issues, and keep your testing process clear and under control.
