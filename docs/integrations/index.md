# Integrations

This section provides an overview of how ReportPortal connects with plugins and external tools used in your testing process. It covers integrations with test frameworks, CI/CD pipelines, and plugins that extend the platform’s functionality.

Here you will find information on how to integrate ReportPortal with popular test frameworks to automatically send test results during execution. These integrations allow you to connect your existing automation setup without changing your workflow, making it easier to centralize results and keep everything in one place.

The section also includes guidance on CI/CD integration, helping you incorporate ReportPortal into your build and deployment pipelines. This allows you to collect and review test results as part of your continuous integration process, improving visibility and making it easier to track quality over time.

In addition, you can find articles about available plugins and how they expand ReportPortal capabilities. Plugins can be used to support additional data processing, reporting options, or connections with third-party services.

Overall, this section helps you understand how to connect ReportPortal with your tools and infrastructure, making your testing process more automated, consistent, and integrated into your development workflow.
