# Administration & Maintenance

This section covers the key aspects of managing and maintaining a ReportPortal instance in a stable and controlled state. It is intended for administrators and technical users responsible for keeping the system reliable, secure, and efficient over time.

Here you will find guidance on routine maintenance tasks, system monitoring, and handling operational issues. The section helps you understand how to keep the platform healthy, how to respond to potential problems, and how to ensure consistent performance as usage grows.

It also provides general recommendations on managing data within the system, including how to control data volume and keep storage under control. In addition, you’ll find information related to system recovery and continuity, helping you prepare for unexpected situations and minimize risks.

Security-related practices are also addressed, giving you an overview of how to manage access and maintain a safe environment.

Overall, this section helps you support the long-term operation of ReportPortal, making it easier to maintain stability, prevent issues, and keep the system running smoothly.
