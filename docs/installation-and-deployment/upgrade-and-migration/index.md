# Upgrade & Migration

**Upgrade & Migration**

This section provides guidance on how to upgrade your ReportPortal instance and migrate it between environments safely and efficiently. It covers the key steps needed to update system components, move data, and ensure everything continues to work as expected after changes.

You’ll find general recommendations on preparing for upgrades, including creating backups, updating dependencies, and verifying system compatibility. The section also explains how to approach migrations, whether you are moving between servers or transferring your setup to a cloud environment.

Special attention is given to preserving important data such as test results, attachments, and analysis information, as well as updating configuration files and integrations after the move. You’ll also learn the importance of validating the system after upgrade or migration to ensure stability and correct functionality.

Overall, this section helps you plan and execute upgrades and migrations with less risk, keeping your ReportPortal environment stable, consistent, and ready for further use and scaling.
