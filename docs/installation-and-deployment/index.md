# Installation & Deployment

The **Installation & Deployment** section explains how to install and deploy the platform in a clear and practical way, covering both the initial setup and further improvements for long-term use.

To get started, it provides a step-by-step guide that helps you install the system and launch it without issues. Before beginning, it’s important to choose the right hardware configuration. Your environment should have enough CPU, memory, and storage, as insufficient resources can lead to slow performance or instability. The platform supports different installation methods, such as Docker and Kubernetes, allowing you to select the option that best fits your infrastructure and level of expertise. The main goal at this stage is to get a working instance up and running as smoothly as possible.

In addition to the basic setup, this section also covers important aspects of maintaining and improving your environment over time. It includes recommendations on keeping your system updated to avoid security risks and performance issues. Monitoring is another key part, as it helps you track system health and quickly detect problems like resource overload or downtime.

You will also find guidance on optimizing your system for larger or more complex projects. This includes tips on scaling, managing data, and improving overall performance. These practices are especially useful as your usage grows and your environment becomes more demanding.

If you need assistance with installation, you can take advantage of our [professional services](https://reportportal.io/pricing/service-packages/).

By following the recommendations in this section, you can not only set up the platform quickly but also ensure it remains stable, efficient, and ready to support your future needs.
