# Organizations

:::info
This is a [premium feature](/premium-features/).
:::

Organizations in ReportPortal introduce a new level of flexibility and control by allowing you to group multiple projects under a two-level hierarchy. This scalable structure simplifies how teams manage and organize their testing efforts, making it easier to support enterprise-level testing at scale.

Organizations come with the next key benefits:

* **Simplified Project Grouping and Test Management**

With Organizations, teams can structure their projects in the way that best fits their workflow – by department, test type, product area, or business unit. This makes it easier to keep related testing efforts together, improving clarity and coordination across large teams and complex environments.

* **One Place for Common Settings**

Now you can apply the data retention settings across multiple projects at once. This reduces repetitive manual work and ensures consistency in how test results are collected and stored – while still allowing flexibility when needed at the individual project level.

Overall, Organizations make it easier to scale your testing operations, reduce administrative overhead, and keep everything structured – whether you're managing five projects or fifty.
